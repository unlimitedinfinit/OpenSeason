# Business

## Business Model
Open Season is a free, open-source, local-first desktop application. There is no direct licensing, subscription, or usage fee. Instead, the application serves as a lead generation funnel and companion client for **JustLegal.me**, which offers paid professional case management, lawyer matching, document review, and legal consultations.

## Pricing Rationale
- **Free & Open Source**: Establishing trust is critical for whistleblowers. By keeping the app free and open-source, we ensure that users can inspect the code to verify that it is truly local-first and contains no telemetry.
- **Referral Channel**: Once a user has compiled and organized their evidence locally, they need professional legal review and filing. Open Season guides them to JustLegal.me to match with lawyers who work on False Claims Act (Qui Tam) cases.

## Revenue Streams

| Stream | % of Revenue | Status |
|---|---|---|
| JustLegal.me Referrals | 100% | Active |
| Software Licensing | 0% | Open Source |

## Strategy
Open Season builds credibility with security-conscious whistleblowers by being open-source and local-first. We establish ourselves as the go-to tool for preparing evidence under the False Claims Act, and convert these users into paid consultations on JustLegal.me when they are ready to file their complaints.

## Goals

| Timeframe | Goal | Metric |
|---|---|---|
| 3 months | Stabilize the Tauri v2 desktop build | Zero reported install errors |
| 6 months | Increase referral clicks to JustLegal.me | 15% click-through rate from dashboard |
